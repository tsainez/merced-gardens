A treasurer chairs the finance committee, manages the budget, and takes the lead on audits.

### Responsibilities

*   **Financial Oversight**: Oversee the management of the financial affairs of the organization, ensuring accurate record-keeping and transparency.
*   **Budgeting**: Lead the annual budget preparation process and monitor the budget throughout the year.
*   **Reporting**: Present financial reports to the Board of Directors at regular meetings, explaining variances and financial health.
*   **Compliance**: Ensure that appropriate financial systems and controls are in place and that the organization files necessary tax forms (e.g., IRS Form 990) and reports on time.
*   **Finance Committee**: Chair the Finance Committee, if one exists, to dive deeper into financial matters and make recommendations to the Board.
*   **Banking**: Manage bank accounts and ensure funds are deposited and dispersed in accordance with board policies.
