A secretary typically schedules board meetings, takes minutes, maintains corporate records, and monitors legal compliance.

### Responsibilities

*   **Meeting Coordination**: Schedule board meetings and notify members in advance. Ensure that meetings are conducted according to the bylaws.
*   **Record Keeping**: Take accurate minutes of all board meetings, recording decisions, motions, and votes. Maintain a secure archive of these minutes.
*   **Corporate Records**: Maintain the organization's key documents, including the Certificate of Incorporation, Bylaws, and Conflict of Interest Policy.
*   **Compliance**: Ensure the organization stays compliant with state and federal filing requirements.
*   **Communication**: Act as a point of contact for board members regarding meeting schedules and official documentation.
*   **Term**: Serve a designated term as defined in the bylaws (typically 1-2 years), with the possibility of re-election.
