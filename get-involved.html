Join Our Board

Merced Botanical Gardens is pre-operational — we are building our founding board right now, and everything about the organization is a work in progress. This is a chance to shape a community institution from its very first days.

Please note: because we are such an early-stage organization, all board positions are strictly volunteer and unpaid.

Donation

Your donations help us grow and maintain the Merced Botanical Gardens. Every contribution makes a difference in preserving local flora and providing a beautiful space for the community.
