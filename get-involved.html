--- layout: page title: "Get Involved" description: "Support the Merced Botanical Gardens through volunteering, donations, and community outreach." --- Community support keeps the Merced Botanical Gardens growing. Whether you have time, resources, or ideas to share, we welcome your partnership. ## Volunteer Help us prepare garden beds, assist with events, or share your expertise in education and outreach. Tell us about your interests and availability so we can match you with upcoming opportunities. ## Donate Contributions fund native plantings, tools, and educational materials. Consider a one-time gift or recurring donation to accelerate our next phase of work. ## Outreach Do you represent a school, community group, or local organization? We would love to collaborate on tours, workshops, and special programs. Contact us to plan something together. Looking for more details? Reach out through our contact form and our team will follow up.
