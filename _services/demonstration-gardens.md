Our Demonstration Gardens serve as a living laboratory and community hub, showcasing sustainable gardening practices and the beauty of our local flora.

# Native Plant Education

We provide immersive educational experiences focused on the importance of native plants in our ecosystem. Visitors can learn about:

*   **Drought Tolerance:** How native plants thrive in our local climate with minimal water.
*   **Biodiversity:** The role of native plants in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
*   **Landscape Design:** Practical ways to incorporate native species into home gardens.

# Master Gardener Clinics

Hosted by certified Master Gardeners, our clinics offer expert advice and hands-on workshops for gardeners of all skill levels. Topics include:

*   **Seasonal Gardening:** Tips for planting and maintenance throughout the year.
*   **Pest Management:** Sustainable methods for controlling pests and diseases.
*   **Soil Health:** Techniques for composting and improving soil fertility.

# Youth Programs

Inspiring the next generation of environmental stewards, our youth programs engage children and teens through interactive learning.

*   **Junior Gardener Club:** Hands-on planting and gardening activities.
*   **Nature Walks:** Guided tours to explore the garden's biodiversity.
*   **Science Projects:** Citizen science initiatives and ecology experiments.
